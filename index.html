About Me Welcome to Finding Harmony Counseling, where I believe in the power of healing through connection, compassion, and professional therapeutic support. My practice is dedicated to helping individuals and couples navigate life's challenges with evidence-based approaches and a holistic understanding of mental health and well-being. I'm Kara Tomlinson, MA, LPCC, and I'm passionate about providing meaningful therapeutic support to those seeking healing and growth. I worked in the medical field for 10+ years because I wanted to help people, but I learned that helping people on a personal level is more fulfilling and wasn't possible through my previous career. This realization led me to pursue my passion for mental health and counseling, where I can connect with individuals on a deeper, more meaningful level and support their journey toward healing and self-discovery.

What therapy with me looks like

In a world where we are increasingly separated from our humanness, I strive to provide a space for folks to reconnect with themselves in a holistic way. I welcome folks seeking support for anxiety, depression, trauma, and relationship challenges. If you're looking for a therapist who will listen and encourage the processing of your experiences, thoughts, and emotions, I could be a great fit for your needs. Whether you're seeking clarity, inner peace, or deeper connections with yourself and your community, I'm here to support you.

I am certified in Trauma and Grief therapy and utilize Mindfulness and Parts therapy. Together, we can work to relieve stress, increase self compassion, and discover your inner strengths in order to live the life you want to live. I offer virtual talk therapy sessions as well as in person sessions in the Denver area.

If you're ready to reconnect with yourself or the natural world around you, I invite you to reach out.
